The rules

THE RULES!

Player attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Rules

An ace is worth 1 or 11. If the card cound goes over 21 for any of the participants, the ace is automatically worth 1, else, it's worth 11.

If a participant's hand has 2 or more aces, only one of these may be worth 1.

Once the player stands, the dealer's face-down card is turned up. If the total is 17 or more, it must stand. If the total is 16 or under, dealer must take a card. The dealer must continue to take cards until the total is 17 or more, or his hand is worth more than player's, at which point the dealer must stand.

The participant who reaches 0$ first, is the one to lose the match.
